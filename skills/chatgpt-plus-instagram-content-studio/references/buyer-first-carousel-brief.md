# Buyer-first carousel brief for Refrimix-style content

Use this brief when creating premium Instagram carousels for buyers rather than installers.

## Audience
- Final buyers and decision-makers.
- Good targets: homeowners, architects, offices, clinics, restaurants, condos, and builders/investors.

## Positioning
- Lead with project quality, comfort, aesthetics, and integration with architecture.
- Frame climatization as a project decision, not just a product purchase.
- Emphasize: load calculation, airflow distribution, ducts, drainage, electrical planning, automation, low noise, and clean visual integration.

## Copy rules
- Avoid installer-centric language unless the user explicitly asks for technical/installation marketing.
- Prefer purchase-intent and project-intent keywords.
- Keep captions premium, direct, and buyer-facing.

## Visual direction
- Editorial, minimal, high-end, calm, spacious, realistic.
- Avoid generic technician/maintenance imagery for premium buyer campaigns.

## Production pattern
- Generate and review one slide/image at a time.
- Confirm the visual direction before advancing to the next slide.
- When the content comes from ChatGPT Plus VNC, verify the generated image in the DOM before treating it as final.
